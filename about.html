Our Story

Founded in 2024, QR Generator Pro was born from a simple observation: while QR codes are everywhere and incredibly useful, creating beautiful, customized ones was unnecessarily complicated and often expensive. We set out to change that.

Our team of passionate developers and designers came together with a shared vision: to democratize QR code creation and make it accessible to everyone, from small business owners to large enterprises, from students to seasoned marketers.

Today, we're proud to offer a powerful yet intuitive tool that helps businesses and individuals create professional QR codes in seconds, completely free of charge, with no hidden fees or watermarks.

About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information. They were invented in 1994 by Denso Wave, a Japanese automotive company, and have become essential tools for bridging physical and digital experiences.
