Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

+

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information, such as URLs, text, contact information, and more. They can be scanned using a smartphone camera to quickly access the encoded information.

How do I create a QR code?

+

Creating a QR code with our generator is simple:

  1. Enter your text or URL in the content field
  2. Customize the appearance using our style options
  3. Add your logo (optional)
  4. Download in your preferred format

What file formats are supported for download?

+

We support multiple download formats:

  • PNG (recommended for most uses)
  • SVG (for vector graphics)
  • JPEG (for photos)
  • WEBP (for web use)
  • PDF (for printing)

Can I add my logo to the QR code?

+

Yes! You can add your logo to the center of the QR code. We recommend using a PNG or SVG file with a transparent background for best results. The logo size and margin can be adjusted to ensure the QR code remains scannable.

What is error correction level?

+

Error correction level determines how much of the QR code can be damaged or obscured while still remaining scannable. We offer four levels:

  • L (Low): 7% recovery capacity
  • M (Medium): 15% recovery capacity
  • Q (Quartile): 25% recovery capacity
  • H (High): 30% recovery capacity

Higher levels are recommended when adding logos or using the QR code in challenging environments.

Is there a limit to how many QR codes I can generate?

+

No, there are no limits! You can generate as many QR codes as you need, completely free of charge. We don't add watermarks or restrict any features.
